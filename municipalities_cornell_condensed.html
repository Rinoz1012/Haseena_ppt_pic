Main Notes
Evolution
Madras Municipal Corporation (1687) = oldest; Bombay & Calcutta corporations (1726)!
Lord Ripon's Resolution (1882) = "Magna Carta of Local Self-Government"!
GoI Act 1919 (dyarchy, transferred subject) → GoI Act 1935 (provincial autonomy)!
74th Amendment Act 1992 (force 1 Jun 1993) finally gave constitutional status!
74th Amendment Act
Part IX-A (243P-243ZG) + 12th Schedule (18 subjects)!
243Q = 3 types (Nagar Panchayat/Council/Corporation) based on population, density, revenue, non-agri employment — Governor decides!
243T = reservation SC/ST + women ≥1/3 including chairperson seats!
243S Wards Committee (mandatory pop>3 lakh) & 243ZE Metropolitan Planning Committee (mandatory pop>10 lakh) — unique to municipalities!
Same SEC (243ZA) and SFC (243Y) as panchayats!
Types of Urban Government
Municipal Corporation (large cities, Mayor+Commissioner) | Municipality/Council (smaller towns)!
Notified Area Committee (fast-growing town, entirely NOMINATED) | Town Area Committee (small town, limited functions)!
Cantonment Board (Defence Ministry, Cantonments Act 2006) | Township (PSU-run) | Port Trust | Special Purpose Agency (parastatal, e.g. Development Authority)!
Municipal Personnel
Mayor = ceremonial head, short term (~1yr, varies by state)!
Municipal Commissioner = real executive authority, state-appointed (IAS/state service)!
CEO system for smaller municipalities; Commissioner system for corporations!
Municipal Revenue
Tax: property tax (main), profession tax, advertisement tax, entertainment tax!
Non-tax: user charges, license fees, rent from property!
Grants: SFC + Union Finance Commission (Art 280)! Octroi largely abolished post-GST, causing fund crunch!
Central Council of Local Government
Established 1954, renamed 1985 as "Central Council of Local Government and Urban Development"!
Chairman = Union Minister (Housing & Urban Affairs); members = state urban development ministers!
Purely advisory body under Art 263 — reforms proposals, best practice exchange!
Summary
Evolution: Madras Corp 1687 → Lord Ripon 1882 → GoI Acts 1919/1935 → 74th Amendment 1992 (force 1993) = Part IX-A + 12th Schedule (18 subjects). 3 types via 243Q; unique features = Wards Committee (pop>3L) & Metropolitan Planning Committee (pop>10L). 8 types of urban govt incl. Corporation, Council, Notified/Town Area Committees, Cantonment Board, Township, Port Trust, Special Purpose Agency. Mayor=ceremonial, Commissioner=real executive. Revenue = property tax + non-tax + SFC/Union FC grants, octroi abolished. Central Council of Local Government (1954/1985) = advisory apex body under Union Housing Minister.
