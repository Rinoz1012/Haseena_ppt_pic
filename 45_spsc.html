Main Notes
Composition & Appointment
SPSC consists of a Chairman and other members, appointed by the GOVERNOR of the state (unlike UPSC, appointed by President)!
At least half the members should have held office for 10+ years under the Government of India or a State!
Term = 6 years or age 65, whichever is earlier!
Removal
Despite being appointed by the Governor, SPSC Chairman/members can be removed ONLY by the PRESIDENT — not the Governor!
Misbehaviour ground requires a Supreme Court inquiry & confirmation before removal!
Other grounds (insolvency, paid employment outside official duties, infirmity of mind/body) allow direct removal by the President without SC inquiry!
Independence
Security of tenure; service conditions cannot be varied to disadvantage after appointment!
Expenditure of SPSC charged on the Consolidated Fund of the State — not subject to annual legislative vote!
Post-retirement restrictions parallel UPSC — designed to prevent the executive from influencing members through post-service incentives!
Functions
Conducts examinations for appointments to State Civil Services!
Advises the state government on recruitment methods, principles for promotion/transfer, disciplinary matters, and legal cost/pension claims of civil servants!
Limitations & Role
Purely advisory — Governor/state government can override its recommendations!
Excludes Group C/D recruitment and reservation policy matters!
Functions as the state-level watchdog of merit-based recruitment, analogous to UPSC's role at the Union level!
Joint State Public Service Commission
Under Art 315(2)-(4), Parliament may by law establish a single JSPSC for two or more states, if their legislatures pass resolutions requesting it!
Members of the JSPSC are appointed by the PRESIDENT (since it serves multiple states, appointment shifts from Governor to President)!
JSPSC performs the same functions as an individual SPSC, but for all the participating states jointly!
Summary
SPSC (Art 315-323) = Chairman+members appointed by Governor (≥1/2 with 10 yrs govt experience), term 6yrs/65yrs — but removable only by the PRESIDENT (misbehaviour needs SC inquiry; other grounds allow direct removal). Independence via tenure security, protected service conditions, expenditure charged on state's Consolidated Fund, and post-retirement bars. Functions mirror UPSC at state level — conducting exams, advising on recruitment/promotion/discipline — purely advisory, excluding Group C/D and reservation matters. JSPSC (Art 315(2)-(4)) = Parliament-created joint commission for 2+ states on their request, with members appointed by the President.
