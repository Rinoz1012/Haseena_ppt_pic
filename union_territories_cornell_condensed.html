Main Notes
Creation of UTs
Art 1 = India = Union of States, UTs, acquired territories!
Parliament forms/alters UTs by simple-majority law under Art 3/4 — NOT a constitutional amendment!
Reasons: political/admin (Delhi, Chandigarh), cultural distinctiveness (Puducherry, Lakshadweep, DNH&DD), strategic importance (A&N Islands)!
8 UTs: Delhi, Puducherry, Chandigarh, A&N, Lakshadweep, J&K, Ladakh, DNHⅅ J&K+Ladakh became UTs from 31 Oct 2019!
Administration of UTs
Art 239: President administers via an Administrator (LG/Chief Commissioner/Administrator) who acts as his AGENT, unlike a state Governor!
Art 239A: Parliament may create a legislature/CoM for Puducherry by law (discretionary)!
Art 240: President's regulations for A&N, Lakshadweep, DNH&DD (UTs without legislature) have the force of a Parliament Act — can even amend/repeal one!
Special Provisions for Delhi
69th Amendment 1991 added Art 239AA/AB, created NCT of Delhi!
Assembly ≤70 members; CoM ≤10% of assembly, headed by CM!
Assembly legislates on State+Concurrent List EXCEPT Police, Public Order, Land (Union/LG only)!
Conflict between Delhi & Parliament law → Parliament's law prevails! LG may reserve bills for President!
GNCTD Amdt 2021: "Government"=LG; mandates LG's prior opinion before executive action — widened LG discretion, sparked controversy!
Advisory Committees / Oversight
MHA = nodal ministry for UT administration!
Puducherry = only other UT with own legislature (since 1963, Art 239A)!
A&N, Lakshadweep, DNH&DD = no legislature, run directly by Administrator!
Chandigarh's Administrator = concurrently Punjab's Governor!
Summary
UT creation via Art 1 + simple-majority law (Art 3/4), not amendment; reasons = political/cultural/strategic. 8 UTs incl. J&K, Ladakh (2019). Administration (Art 239) = President via Administrator (agent, not Governor-equivalent). Art 239A = discretionary legislature for Puducherry; Art 240 = President's regulations (force of Parliament Act) for legislature-less UTs. Delhi (69th Amdt, Art 239AA/AB) = 70-member Assembly, CM-led CoM, barred from Police/Public Order/Land, Parliament's law prevails on conflict; GNCTD Amdt 2021 expanded LG's role. MHA is nodal ministry; Puducherry is the only other UT with a legislature.
