Main Notes
Establishment
National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) was established under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 — a statutory body serving as the apex authority for disaster management in India!
Its creation was prompted by the inadequacies exposed in India's disaster response during the 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami!
Composition
Chairperson = Prime Minister!
Other members, not exceeding 9, are nominated by the Chairperson, one of whom is designated as Vice-Chairperson!
Objectives
To lay down policies, plans, and guidelines for disaster management to ensure timely and effective response to disasters!
To coordinate the enforcement and implementation of disaster management policy across the country, and ensure that ministries/departments undertake necessary prevention and mitigation measures!
Functions
Lays down policies on disaster management, and approves the National Plan and plans of various central ministries/departments!
Lays down guidelines to be followed by State Authorities in drawing up their own state plans!
Coordinates implementation of national disaster management policy, and recommends provision of funds for mitigation and preparedness measures!
Provides support to other countries affected by major disasters, as decided by the Central Government!
Takes necessary measures for prevention, mitigation, preparedness, and capacity building in relation to any threatening disaster situation!
Additional Function
NDMA is empowered to constitute the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF), a specialized force for disaster response, and the National Institute of Disaster Management (NIDM), for training and capacity building in disaster management!
State Disaster Management Authority (SDMA)
Chairperson = Chief Minister of the state!
Lays down the state disaster management policy, approves the state plan, coordinates its implementation, recommends provision of funds for mitigation measures, and reviews the state's preparedness!
District Disaster Management Authority (DDMA)
Chairperson = District Collector/District Magistrate/Deputy Commissioner (in some states, an elected local representative serves as co-chairperson)!
Acts as the planning, coordinating, and implementing body for disaster management at the district level, and prepares the district disaster management plan!
Summary
NDMA (Disaster Management Act, 2005) was created post-2004 tsunami as India's apex statutory disaster management body, chaired by the PM with up to 9 members. Objectives/functions center on laying down policy, approving national/ministry-level plans, guiding state authorities, coordinating implementation, and recommending mitigation funds; it also constitutes the NDRF and NIDM for response capability and training. This 3-tier structure continues at the state level via SDMA (chaired by the CM) and district level via DDMA (chaired by the District Collector/Magistrate), each responsible for disaster management planning and coordination within their jurisdiction.
