Main Notes
5th Schedule — Scheduled Areas
Applies to 10 states (not NE states under 6th Schedule): AP, Telangana, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, HP, MP, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan!
President declares/alters Scheduled Area by order, after consulting the Governor!
Governor has special responsibility, reports to President annually on administration!
Governor can direct that a Parliament/State law not apply, or apply with modifications, to the area!
Governor makes regulations (with President's assent) restricting land transfer & regulating money-lending!
Tribes Advisory Council (TAC) mandatory: ≤20 members, ¾ tribal MLAs, advises on tribal welfare!
6th Schedule — Tribal Areas
Applies only to Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura, Mizoram!
Grants greater autonomy via elected Autonomous District Councils (ADC)!
ADC = ≤30 members (26 elected 5yr term + 4 Governor-nominated)!
Legislative powers: land, forest, jhum cultivation, village admin, marriage/customs (Governor's assent needed)!
Judicial powers: constitutes Village Councils/Courts for petty tribal cases!
Governor decides formation/reorganization of districts, can annul or suspend ADC acts against public interest!
State-wise: Assam-3 ADCs (Karbi Anglong, Dima Hasao, Bodoland), Meghalaya-3 (Khasi, Jaintia, Garo Hills), Tripura-1, Mizoram-3 (Chakma, Mara, Lai)!
Core Distinction
5th Schedule = Governor-centric, discretionary powers, no separate elected tribal body!
6th Schedule = elected councils with real legislative/judicial/financial powers = significantly more self-governance!
Summary
5th Schedule (10 states) = President declares Scheduled Areas after consulting Governor; Governor has special responsibility (reports to President, modifies laws, regulates land/money-lending); mandatory advisory TAC. 6th Schedule (Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura, Mizoram only) = greater autonomy via elected ADCs (≤30 members) with legislative, judicial, financial powers, subject to Governor's assent/oversight (incl. power to annul). Core distinction: 5th = Governor-discretion model with no elected body; 6th = elected self-governing councils = more autonomy.
