Main Notes
Evolution
BRM 1957 Democratic decentralization, 3-tier, NDC-approved 1958, launched Rajasthan (Nagaur) 1959!
Ashok Mehta 1977 2-tier (ZP+Mandal), first to recommend constitutional status!
GVK Rao 1985 "Grass without roots" — recommended district-level planning + DRDA!
Singhvi 1986 Constitutional status + Gram Sabha as base of democracy!
64th Bill 1989 failed in RS → 73rd Amendment 1992 succeeded, force 24 Apr 1993!
73rd Amendment Act
Part IX (243-243O) + 11th Schedule (29 subjects)!
Gram Sabha(243A)=whole electorate | 3-tier(243B, exception pop<20L)!
Direct election all seats(243C) | Reservation SC/ST+women≥1/3(243D)!
5-year fixed term(243E) | SEC(243K) independent of ECI | SFC(243I) every 5yrs!
Compulsory vs Voluntary
Compulsory Gram Sabha, 3-tier, direct election, reservation, 5yr term, SEC, SFC!
Voluntary OBC reservation, MP/MLA representation, GP chairperson mode, extent of devolution, taxation powers — hence variation across states!
PESA Act 1996
Extends Part IX with modifications to 5th Schedule Scheduled Areas (10 states)!
Gram Sabha approves plans, identifies beneficiaries, controls minor forest produce+minerals+money-lending!
Mandatory Gram Sabha consultation before land acquisition in Scheduled Areas!
Finances
Own tax/non-tax revenue (weak) + State Finance Commission transfers + Union Finance Commission grants (Art 280) + CSS funds!
Panchayats remain heavily dependent on state/central transfers!
Reasons — Ineffective Performance
3 Fs: inadequate Funds, incomplete devolution of Functions, shortage of Functionaries!
Political interference by MPs/MLAs, social dominance (caste/patriarchy, sarpanch-pati), bureaucratic resistance, irregular elections, weak capacity-building!
Summary
Evolution: BRM→Ashok Mehta→GVK Rao→Singhvi→64th Bill(fail)→73rd Amendment 1992 (Part IX + 11th Schedule). Compulsory = Gram Sabha, 3-tier, elections, reservation, term, SEC, SFC. Voluntary = OBC reservation, devolution extent, taxation. PESA 1996 extends to 5th Schedule areas with empowered Gram Sabha. Finance = weak own revenue + SFC/Union FC transfers. Ineffectiveness = 3 Fs (Funds/Functions/Functionaries) + political/social/bureaucratic factors.
