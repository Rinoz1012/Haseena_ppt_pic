Main Notes
Composition
Art 324 vests power of superintendence, direction, control of elections in the Election Commission of India (ECI)!
Consists of a Chief Election Commissioner (CEC) and such number of other Election Commissioners as the President may fix!
Currently a 3-member body (1 CEC + 2 ECs) — multi-member since October 1993!
Covers elections to Parliament, State Legislatures, and the offices of President & Vice-President — NOT local body elections (that's the State Election Commission)!
Appointment & Independence
CEC & ECs appointed by the President!
Under the Chief Election Commissioner and Other Election Commissioners (Appointment, Conditions of Service and Term of Office) Act, 2023, appointments are made on the recommendation of a Selection Committee comprising the PM, Leader of Opposition in Lok Sabha, and a Union Cabinet Minister nominated by the PM!
This 2023 Act followed the Supreme Court's Anoop Baranwal judgment (2023), which had temporarily mandated a committee including the CJI pending a law by Parliament!
Service conditions of CEC/ECs cannot be varied to their disadvantage after appointment — safeguards independence!
Regional Commissioners may be appointed to assist ECI, after consultation with the Commission!
Term & Removal
Term = 6 years, or up to age 65, whichever is earlier!
CEC can be removed only in the same manner as a Supreme Court judge (impeachment by Parliament) — strong protection!
Other ECs can be removed by the President only on the recommendation of the CEC!
Powers & Functions
Determines electoral constituencies' delimitation, prepares & revises electoral rolls!
Conducts elections, fixes election schedule/dates, enforces the Model Code of Conduct (MCC)!
Grants recognition to political parties, allots election symbols, resolves disputes over party splits/mergers!
Advises President/Governor on questions of disqualification of sitting MPs/MLAs!
Advises President on whether elections can be held during a national emergency!
Requisitions staff necessary for conducting elections; can cancel polls in case of booth capturing/large-scale malpractice!
Vision, Mission & Principles
Vision — to build an electoral ecosystem that empowers citizens through free, fair, transparent, inclusive, and participative elections!
Mission — ensuring a level playing field for all political parties/candidates, enhancing ease of voting, and using technology for accessible, error-free processes!
Core principles — political neutrality, transparency, and citizen-centric conduct of elections!
Summary
Election Commission (Art 324) = permanent, independent body superintending elections to Parliament, State Legislatures, President, VP (not local bodies). Composition = CEC + ECs (currently 3-member); appointed by President on Selection Committee's recommendation (PM+LOP+Cabinet Minister) under the 2023 Act, following the Anoop Baranwal judgment. Term = 6 years/65 yrs; CEC removed like a SC judge, other ECs removed on CEC's recommendation. Functions = delimitation, electoral rolls, MCC enforcement, party recognition/symbol allotment, advising President/Governor on MP/MLA disqualification. Vision/Mission centers on free, fair, transparent, inclusive elections with a level playing field for all.
